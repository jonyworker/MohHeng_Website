Our Infrastructures

Our warehouse capacity of 400,000 sq ft across both Sabah and Sarawak is one of the largest and most progressive in the region. We are one of the pioneers companies in East Malaysia to employ an Advanced Warehouse Management System to ensure ease of identification, rapid and seamless tracking.

LAN environment for up to date inventory information

Paperless picking for efficiency

LSystematic BIN routing for increased productivity

Barcode scanning for handling both bulk and loose quantity orders

Intuitive interfaces for short training time and ease of application

Temperature-controlled environment for ideal quality conditions

From here, our products transition to our fleet of vehicles.

The whole process is overseen by Moh Heng from initial receipt to final distribution because we know that mistakes cost money, especially when orders are being sent to the far corners of Sabah and Sarawak. Our systems ensure that our goods arrive in the exact place, time and condition that they were meant to.
