Our longevity in the business is the foundation of our in-depth market knowledge and developed network.

Established in Miri in 1948, Moh Heng has expanded to provide full branch operations in every major city in East Malaysia, working with a broad range of partners.

Our assets are our history, partners, network and people.

Vision

Our goal is to bring the best, global and local FMCG brands in peak condition into every home, bridging business between brand owners and retailers in Malaysia and beyond.

Mission

We aim to promote our FMCG partnerships through professional management, a people-centric approach and appropriate infrastructure to create best value for all.
